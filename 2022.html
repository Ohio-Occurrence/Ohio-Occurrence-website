Overview

2022 was our first Occurrence.


September 1st


Case Farms vigil

Case Farms is a poultry "processing" plant, aka murder house. We held a vigil for all the chickens whose lives are taken from them.



Stark County Fair protest

We protested the Stark County Fair, which is not so fair for the animals.



Stahl's No Harm Farm volunteer

We volunteered at a sancutary.




September 2nd


Cleveland Zoo protest

We protested the animal prison, aka zoo.



Cube of Truth

We did an Anonymous for the Voiceless Cube of Truth, holding non-vegans accountable.



Tiffany&Co fur protest

It takes fifty animals to make one fur coat, so we protested a store that sells them.



September 3rd


Newport Aquarium protest

Newport Aquarium imprisons and exploits thousands of innocent marine animals, so we protested them.



Beach Cleanup

We cleaned up the beach in collaboration with Sea Sheperd.



Edgewater Park giveaway

Who doesn't like free food? We gave some away while leafletting and outreaching.
