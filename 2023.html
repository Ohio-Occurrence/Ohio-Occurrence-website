Overview

2023 was an eventful year, with lots of major actions performed, some of which we can't talk about here. See below for more details on what we can talk about.


September 1st


City Town Hall protest

Working with Slaughter Free Network, we protested at the city Town Hall.



Mt Healthy Hatchery protest

We protested this local chick hatchery that sells entire living, breathing beings for $9 a piece.



Ideal Image disruption

LVMH sold fur. We thought that was wrong.



September 2nd


Jones Fish Hatchery protest

We protested the Jones Fish Hatchery.



Belterra Park protest

We protested the cruelty that is horse racing.



Starbucks disruption/protest

We protested the Starbucks upcharge.



A few activists went inside.



Foreverland Farm sancutary visit

We visited the Foreverland Farm sanctuary and had a cook-out.




September 3rd


Newport Aquarium protest

Newport Aquarium needs no introduction. They cage thousands of animals so that people can gawk at them, often removing them from their natural habitats if not breeding them.



Cincinnati Zoo protest

Just like the aquarium, this place imprisons animals to be stared at by strangers all day.



UC protest

We protested against animal testing at the University of Cincinnati.



Cube of Truth

We did an Anonymous for the Voiceless Cube of Truth, holding non-vegans accountable.
